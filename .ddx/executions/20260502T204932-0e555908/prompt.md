BLOCK aborts the merge; the worktree stays preserved per existing conventions for human resolution.&#xA;- Bypass: bead-level annotation checks_bypass: [{name: &lt;check&gt;, reason: &lt;text&gt;, bead: &lt;bead-id&gt;}] recorded in events.
