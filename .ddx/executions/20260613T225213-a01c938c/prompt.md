PROBLEM&#xA;&#xA;During dogfood on 2026-06-13, one `ddx work --once` attempt for bead ddx-0be85773 reported active route `claude/sonnet`, but the worker process retained multiple direct provider CLI children at the same time: `codex --no-alt-screen` (PID 1875100, older than 7 minutes), `claude --print ... --model sonnet` (PID 1882389, active implementation route), and later `gemini` (PID 1890368).
